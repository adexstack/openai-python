

Recipe Title: Savory Yam Hash

Ingredients:
- 1 large yam, peeled and diced
- 2 eggs
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 tomato, diced
- 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions:
1. Preheat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add the olive oil to the skillet and heat until shimmering.
3. Add the diced yam to the skillet and cook for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the yam is soft and lightly browned.
4. Add the diced onion and tomato to the skillet and cook for another 5 minutes.
5. Crack the eggs into the skillet and stir to combine.
6. Cook for an additional 3-5 minutes until the eggs are cooked through.
7. Serve the savory yam hash immediately. Enjoy!
